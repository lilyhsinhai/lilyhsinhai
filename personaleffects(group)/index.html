Your feet and toes, my love, are like the sun,
Warm and bright, my heart they've won,
They guide me through the path of life,
Through every joy and every strife.

Each toe, so perfect in its own way,
A little world to love and play,
A world I cherish, a world I hold,
A world of love, more precious than gold.

Your feet, so gentle, so soft and sweet,
A soothing balm, to my tired feet,
They wrap around me, with tender care,
A love so deep, beyond compare.

And when the day is done, my dear,
And we rest our feet, without a fear,
We know that they will be, forevermore,
Our faithful guide, our anchor, our shore.

So let us cherish, your feet and toes,
For they are the source, of love that grows,
Let us be grateful, for all they give,
For in them, we find, the reason to live.
